About our firm:

Biofabrication is transforming the fashion industry by offering a sustainable and eco-friendly approach to clothing production. We are proud to use Merino wool for our clothing as it offers superior comfort, is cost-effective and has a low carbon footprint. Our shoe soles are made of algae foam derived from algae biomass, a durable material that is environmentally friendly. We also use renewable cork and linen for our laces, which are biodegradable. By using these innovative materials, we're able to create stylish and sustainable fashion products that contribute to a greener future.
